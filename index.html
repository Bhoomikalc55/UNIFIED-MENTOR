EAGLE

OWL

PARROT
Parrots are colorful and intelligent birds known for their vibrant plumage and impressive ability to mimic sounds and human speech. Their strong beaks and dexterous talons make them adept at foraging and manipulating objects in their environment.

HERON
Herons are graceful wading birds with long legs and necks, renowned for their patience and precision in hunting fish and aquatic prey. Their elegant flight and striking appearance make them a captivating presence in wetlands and waterways.
